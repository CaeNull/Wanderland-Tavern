General

  1. Keymeras are a closed species. This means that you may not create one if you do not have a MYO Slot, which can be obtained from events, raffles, server activities that reward species currency, and USD sales.
  2. This species is 18+. You must be above the age of 18 to interact with this species, and if we find out otherwise, you will be banned from our community and your Keymera will be sentenced to dismantling, thus voided.
  3. If you want to follow our lore, it is much appreciated and very encouraged! This is because most of the events will be lore-based. It doesn’t have to be perfectly following, but please familiarize yourself with our world.
  4. Any designs made to promote hateful speech, offensive stereotypes, or using race or culture as an 'aesthetic' will not be tolerated.
  5. Please inform a mod in the server if a Keymera is traded/sold/gifted, so that the masterlist can stay up to date. You should also inform us of any username changes or if you move accounts! Additionally, you may contact a staff member with any concerns or other issues that may occur, provided they are related to the species.
  6. Do not talk about heavy or commonly triggering topics in public spaces, such as in the server. A warning may be issued by staff for this depending on severity.
  7. NSFW must be properly tagged and censored when shared in public spaces or kept to the appropriate discord channels.
  8. Do not trace/copy existing art or knowingly use any AI in production of art!
  9. Overall, respect everyone in the Keymera community and treat members and non-members with kindness!

Payments and Processing

  1. Payment rules are up to the individual artist.
  2. MYOs and designs have a cooldown of 1 week before they can transfer ownership.
  3. Please respect the individual artist’s TOS and preferences!
  4. Backing out is not allowed, so please do not delete comments or ghost payments. Only bid on what you can afford.
  5. If a profile is hidden, you must provide staff with an access key.
  6. Sellers reserve the right to refuse service to anyone.

Ownership

  1. Ownership is transferred upon receipt of full payment and the design becomes irrevocable unless it was acquired through unethical methods.
  2. All designs are for non-commercial purposes only.
  3. You may co-own a design with one (1) other member, however, it is not up to the mods to fix or mediate any disagreements between co-owners.
  4. The original designer should always be credited for the design.

Ownership Transfers

  1. To transfer to a user, you must fill out the transfer form on the Discord server. It will be transferred by an admin ASAP.
  2. Upon trading, reselling or gifting the design to another individual, the previous owner agrees to transfer all ownership rights to the recipient.

MYO Sales

  1. All slots have a 7-DAY COOLDOWN, meaning that you cannot trade/resell/gift your MYO slots until 7 days have passed. This also applies to slots from events, raffles, and purchases!
  2. If anyone is reselling a MYO for more than its listed value, please contact staff as soon as possible.
  3. Adoptables and MYO slots cannot be refunded, even in the event of blacklisting.
  4. Slots from SHOP CURRENCY, RAFFLES, or EVENTS cannot be sold, only traded or gifted.
  5. Slots from REAL LIFE CURRENCY SALES can be traded, gifted, or resold.
  6. Individuals may purchase 1 slot per month. (You can still acquire slots from sources such as raffles, events, species currency, etc.) There is only a limit on the number of Irreplaceable Keymera slots available per month, though it is subject to change.
  7. PPayments for slots and adoptables must be sent to the seller within forty-eight (48) hours. Holds and payment plans are allowed and are to be discussed with the designer or staff in advance.
  8. MYO sales will be advertised on the TH world and Discord server whenever open!

Editing or Redesigning A Keymera

    If it is your own design, you can change large parts of the design including traited items as long as it is within the same rarity and you have the appropriate shop item.

    Redesigns are allowed using the following criteria:

    • In the case of an adopt or custom, you must have the original artist and/or designer's permission to redesign the Keymera.
    • You must follow that artist's redesign TOS.
    • Redesigns must keep all traits of the previous Masterlist design and visually resemble the original design by at least 80% .
    • The only exception to the above is if you have the specific redesign shop item.

    These rules are in place to keep the original design from becoming unrecognizable, and to try and protect the original artists intentions. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to DM an owner or mod through toyhouse or discord, or ask in the questions channel of the server.

Redesign Terms

  1. These changes are considered redesigns and MUST be submitted for reapproval:
    • Adding/tweaking/removing TRAITED characteristics.
  2. Traited characteristics include the keys, keyhole, faceted traits, and others that are unique to the Keymera species.
  3. Minor redesigns do not need to be re-approved, though you may have to request a masterlist update for the image or new reference.
    Minor changes include, but are not limited to:
    • Hair (style, color, length, etc.)
    • Outfits. Unless you want us to update the Masterlist image.
    • Eye color.
    • Hairstyle edits.
    • Updating/Redrawing the reference image without changing any traits. Unless you want us to update the Masterlist image.
    • Slight color tweaks- this means going from one shade of blue to a similar one, not changing the keys from red to blue.
    • Simplifying a design element OUTSIDE of traits. Such as simplifying heavily patterned clothing, complicated hairstyles, etc.

Design Voiding

  1. Official Keymera or those created by staffs or GAs may not be voided.
  2. Designs can be voided completely from the species at the original designer's discretion. Once voided, they can not be reinstated.
  3. You may be asked to remove other identifying traits on a case-by-case basis (such as traits from trait packs).
    If you did not design the MYO Keymera that you’re voiding, you must provide proof of contacting the original creator for permission to void it from the species.
  4. The unedited Keymera version of a voided Keymera may not be gifted, traded, or sold after voiding.
  5. Voided Keymera are completely separate from the species and may not be used in any manner within the community.
  6. If the design was created by you: You have full rights to the design and can do whatever you'd like with it.
  7. If the design was created by someone else: The design will follow the original designer's TOS.
  8. Voided Keymera are no longer subject to nor protected by our TOS.
